Events

We run a number of events each year for the benefit of our members.
Here are some examples from the last few years that we hope to continue into 2025:

Weekly Meetups

Every week during semester we run a meetup at the Sandy Bay campus as well as online on Discord where members can chill out after a busy week, discuss goings-on and work together of various club and personal projects. Snacks included! We hope to have Launceston meetups in the future, please let us know if you would like to attend or even help out with that! In 2025, our meetups run from 2:30 PM to 4:30 PM on Thursdays in Centenary 140.

BBQs

We have held public barbeques on the Sandy Bay campus since 2023 with cheap food available and even discounts for members. We cater to as many people as possible by offering both meat and vegan options as well as soft drinks. They are one of the events where the Club engages with the wider student community.

UTAS Clubs and Societies Days

The Programming Club has run stalls at the University’s Clubs and Societies days since 2022 and the new Mini Clubs and Societies day since it began last year. We use these events to talk to students about our events and show off the projects that members have worked on in years prior. We will be at the upcoming Clubs and Societies day in Sandy Bay on the 26th of Feburary, hopefully we will see you there!

Game Jams

We are proud to send Club members to various game jam events across the past few years.

Game Nights

We plan to run a combined games night with the Maths and Physics Society in Semester 1 2025, as well as host our own games night sometime throughout the year.

General Meetings

Our general meetings are held to discuss broader club matters such as significant club decisions and the election of officers. All Club members are invited to attend and may vote in proceedings. There are two types of general meeting: our annual general meeting; and a special general meeting, which can be called at any time by the Committee.

Movie Nights
